COUSCOUS
NORTHEASTERN

C
ouscous is a tipicall northeastern food, mainly because your low-cust (we need few ingredients to make) and flavor.
You will know when this delicious food is ready by its smell! Takes about 35 minutes to prepare!

Ingredients

  • Corn flakes (500g / ~17,5oz)

  • Salt (One spoon)

  • The couscous cooking pot


Steps

  1. Put the couscous in a bowl and you need to soak the couscous

  2. Allow it to rest for 15 minutes

  3. While it, put until three fingers of water in the couscous cook. pot

  4. After 15 minutes, put the couscous in the couscous cooking pot

  5. Wait about 15 minutes too, and you couscous is ready to eat! (wait few minutes for it cool down)
