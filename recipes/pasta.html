he spaghetti pasta It's a famous food originated from Italy, also my favorite food because 1. It's simple to cooking 2. It's low-cust 3. You can add many spices (and should!). This recipe takes about 20 minutes, so, let's go:

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed

  • 21 oz / 600g crushed tinned tomatoes

  • 2 tbsp chilli flakes

  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley


Steps:

  1. Heat oil over medium high heat

  2. Add garlic, stir until fragrant

  3. Then add the tinned tomatoes and chilli flakes

  4. Simmer on medium for 5 minutes
